Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire, connect, and empower technology leaders and enthusiasts around the world. We believe in the power of innovation to solve real-world problems and strive to make our events inclusive, forward-thinking, and impactful.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Maya Lin

A leading AI researcher, Dr. Lin is known for her groundbreaking work in ethical machine learning and inclusive AI systems. Her keynote at TechCon 2022 emphasized human-centered AI development.

Alan Rivera

As the CTO of a major cloud computing firm, Alan Rivera shared insights on scaling infrastructure for the future of work and real-time collaboration at TechCon 2021.

Sophia Deng

A tech policy advocate and cybersecurity expert, Sophia Deng spoke at TechCon 2019 on the intersection of privacy, governance, and open technology.
